Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, popularly known as Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, was a visionary scientist and the 11th President of India. He was born on October 15, 1931, and passed away on July 27, 2015. Dr. Kalam made significant contributions to India's space and missile programs and was known as the "Missile Man of India."

He was not only a brilliant scientist but also an inspiring teacher and a great motivator for the youth. Dr. Kalam's life and work continue to inspire millions of people worldwide. His vision and dedication to the progress of science and education have left an indelible mark on the world.
